What is the Geneous Project?

The Geneous Project aims to raise awareness about the impact of Genetically Modified Organisms, in the context of health, our environment and evolution. The technology, side effects and economic impacts will be discovered.

What are GMOs?

Genetically Modified Organisms (GMOs) are organisms whose genetic material has been altered to fit a specific set of features or genotypes - one must isolate the genes they insert into the host organism and combine it with other genetic elements. While GMOs present visual and economic benefits, the products of these engineering processes are harmful to human health, ecosystems, organic farms and evolution.

The Issue

While GMOs present visual and economic benefits, the products of these engineering processes are harmful to human health, ecosystems, organic farms and the evolution of plant species. GM products' most prominent issues are their capability to escape in wild populations, the persistence of the gene after harvestation and frequent attacks on non-target organisms and ecosystems.
